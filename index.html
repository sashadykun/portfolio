About Me

Hi! I’m Sasha. Frontend Web Developer and Information Technology fan. Specializing in developing custom desktop and mobile apps.

Who are you, Sasha Dykun?

I'm a cumulation of a Web Developer, IT Admin, Out-of-Box Thinker, Eager Traveler, Unstoppable Team Player and Proactive Team Leader at the same time. Interesting combination, isn’t it?
And besides I know how to deal with all those things, when and where to use one or another part of me. Useful ability, don’t you agree?

Why I can be a good part of your team?

I've spent half of my life traveling and living in different parts of the world: Ukraine, England, Norway, Australia, Georgia, Tunisia, USA etc.

Those experiences gave me a big strength – to understand people from different cultures, generations and professions.
I've got the internal assurance that there is an approach to any person and any job can be done, if we create a plan and are not afraid to fail, rescue ourselves and fail again. And it can repeat a lot of times, till you reach the limit. Yes. There is always a limit of failures.

What can I do?

I’ve learned and practiced Linux Administration for quite a long time. I know how system works, how it can be configured and fixed and how it can be crashed I know too ??
Web Development has geared into my life like Hennessey Venom F5 at 301mph. And yes – so fast, beautifully and making my life impossible without it.
LearningFuze was that platform where this dream was waiting for me.
Now I can proudly say I’m FrontEnd Web Developer that specializes in developing custom desktop and mobile applications, knows how to create impeccable functionality, responsive framework, add features, fix bugs and many many more.

The last but not the least...
I’ve had a long path to put my foot on the way of Development Field but each and every step was worth it: meeting different people, hours of talking and sharing ideas, learning OS administration, working in the web of servers racks, networking and virtual machines, studying, attending meetups, completing projects, taking additional online courses, absorbing hours of videos and article reading. Now it’s all in me, only developing and getting strength. As one my good friend says “Practice makes perfect”! And as I say “It’s all a path. Enjoy it every moment.”
