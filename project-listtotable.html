List To Table

Few-clicks application that helps to save your time when you need to get perfect place for your perfect breakfast,lunch or dinner. Just choose the spot, enter your party size and phone number and here you go!
Your table is waiting for you line-free!

Front-end: ReactJS, JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5, Axios, Regex, Redux, MaterializeCSS, Node.js, Google maps API, Twilio API
Back-end: Ubuntu 18, Apache, PHP, MySQL
